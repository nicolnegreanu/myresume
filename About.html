My mood carries me like a vine, or a leaf in the wind, from low to high and from high to low, from soft to hard and vice versa. It carries me like a stork on a cabbage...

In the eternity of a moment, the echo of thought flashes through me,

Then it fades with the "axe of a paddle" of oblivion,

But in its wake remains the emotion of grief, of despair,

So that I no longer know what the starting point was...

Reflection on resignation

The bittersweet taste of resignation saves me from suffering, or, more precisely, if i do not avoid suffering, i will go through it, like a goose through water, then i will see that it is good. An objective, non-relative good, which leaves no room for interpretations, contrary to when i build my happiness on the suffering of others. Resignation is composed of mercy, but, above all, it brings a lot of peace of mind, which makes it easy to bear, and in the end you even receive a reward - and what a reward: "but he who endures to the end, the same will be saved".

Let's say that living with resignation is similar to going through life like a duck through radioactive water...
